Culture

Moroccan culture is a rich tapestry woven from Arab, Berber, and European influences. Known for its vibrant music like Andalusian and Gnawa styles, Morocco hosts colorful festivals such as the Marrakech International Film Festival and the Moussem of Tan-Tan. The country's intricate craftsmanship, from zellige tiles to traditional carpets, showcases its artistic heritage, while its warm hospitality remains a cornerstone of Moroccan life..

Explore the traditions of Moroccan tea ceremonies, the rhythmic beats of Gnawa music, and the artistic beauty of traditional Moroccan Zellige tiles.
