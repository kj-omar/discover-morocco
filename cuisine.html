Cuisine

Moroccan cuisine is renowned for its bold flavors and the masterful use of spices like saffron, cumin, and cinnamon. Signature dishes such as savory tagines, sweet and flaky pastillas, and hearty couscous reflect the country's rich culinary heritage. Each meal is a celebration of tradition, often accompanied by a refreshing glass of mint tea.

Don't miss trying a cup of traditional Moroccan mint tea, a symbol of hospitality and culture.
