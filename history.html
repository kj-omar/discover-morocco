History

Morocco boasts a rich and diverse history shaped by Berber, Arab, and European influences. Its legacy spans ancient dynasties like the Almoravids and Almohads, whose architectural marvels still stand today. As a key hub of trade and culture, Morocco has played a vital role in bridging Africa, Europe, and the Middle East. Its fascinating past continues to shape its modern identity, blending tradition with progress.

Explore the remnants of the Almoravid dynasty, the beauty of Andalusian architecture, and Morocco's journey to independence.
